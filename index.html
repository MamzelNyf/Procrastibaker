Procrastibaking

The practice of baking something completely unnecessary, with the intention of avoiding “real” work

Hi, I'm Fanny

Positive, hard-working and an insatiable learner, I like to define myself as a Web Designer. But I'm not only a job title.
I'm a Foodie and I'm lucky enough to have a Chef at home who is cooking amazing dishes.
I love Nature, hiking in beautiful and preserved places provides me with such a positive feeling and powerful energy. Caring for our Environment is part of my everyday routine.
I love Colours, Layout, Photography, Painting, Digital manipulation... I think we all have gifts to make Life prettier.
I'm a Cat lover and I can't wait to have a permanent situation to be able to take care of one (or many). For now, I'm just melting each time I watch videos and photos of cats.

I have started She{Codes} Plus program in June 2020, to develop my knowledge in web development, meet passionate women with similar interests and build a tech network in Perth.
But most of it, it is another step in my new life in Australia and I'm excited to find a community I belong to.
I realised a few months ago that I was missing an important thing from France (besides my family): a Crunchy French style Baguette. I have started reading blogs, watching videos, following tutorials... Baking is actually like learning a new programming language! Step-by-step, by practising, it becomes easier and better.
Let me share with you an enjoyable way to escape from the computer and satisfy a craving for crunch: Procratisbaking!
